Yams are fascinating plants with a unique biology and growth cycle. Unlike many other root crops, yams have a long and distinct period of dormancy and a climbing growth habit that requires careful management. In this chapter, we'll examine the yam life cycle, how they are propagated, and the challenges involved in breeding new and improved varieties.
